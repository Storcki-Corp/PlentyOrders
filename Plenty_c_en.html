Storcki Corp is a young video game development studio, born from the meeting of its five members at the EPITA School of Computer Engineering in Strasbourg. Although the members of Storcki Corp come from diverse backgrounds, it is now the city of Strasbourg, the capital of the Grand Est region, that connects them.


Thus, the symbol of Alsace, the stork, naturally finds its place in the studio's logo. In folklore, storks are associated with delivery and mobility. The logo, and more specifically the stork, symbolizes Storcki Corp's ability to deliver high-quality video games worldwide.


Storcki Corp maintains a balance between originality, technical precision, and a touch of humor. The shared values of the members of Storcki Corp, such as mutual support, inclusivity, and a family spirit, shape the company from within and are expressed through their achievements.
